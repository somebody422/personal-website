What are you wearing right now?

Wait, that was a little creepy. Let me reword that.

Think about the outfit you’re currently wearing. What does your outfit say about you?

Often times (whether right or wrong) people make a first impression based on the clothes you wear.

They make assumptions about things like your style, your personality, your age, or even the type of impression you want to make.

There are also different outfits for different occasions.

For example, you wouldn’t wear a bathing suit to a job interview.

The same can be said about the fonts you choose to use in your branding and in your website's design.
